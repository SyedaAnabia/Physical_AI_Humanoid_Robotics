# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Overview
This repository is dedicated to the study and development of **Physical Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and **Humanoid Robotics**. It covers theoretical concepts, practical implementations, and learning resources to help students and enthusiasts understand the field of robotics, AI, and human-like machines.

Humanoid robots are machines designed to replicate human motions and behavior, often incorporating AI to interact with their environment intelligently. Physical AI focuses on combining computational intelligence with real-world physical systems.

---

## Features
- **Humanoid Robot Design**: Study of mechanical design, actuators, and sensors.
- **Physical AI Implementation**: Integrating AI algorithms for perception, decision-making, and control.
- **Robotics Projects**: Step-by-step examples and tutorials for building robots.
- **Simulation & Testing**: Code examples and simulations to test humanoid behaviors virtually.

## Introduction
Humanoid robotics combines mechanics, electronics, and AI to create machines that mimic human movements and intelligence. Physical AI allows these machines to interact with their environment, adapt to changes, and make decisions based on sensory input.

This repository provides learning materials, code examples, and documentation to guide you from beginner to advanced levels in robotics and AI.
